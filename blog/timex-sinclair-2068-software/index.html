Timex/Sinclair 2068 Software

Timex Computer Corporation produced a number of programs for the TS 2068. Most were by American programmers, some were ports of Spectrum programs.

Timex worked with Softsync and other vendors to source many of the programs. Software was available on cassette or Command Cartridge.

Note: Timex may have gone through several names for their cartridges. Casino 1 is listed as an “Insta-Load™” cartridge in the Winter 1983 catalog; all other cartridges are “Command Cartridge™”.
