User Groups

User groups a popular way to meet with other Sinclair and Timex computer owners, exchange ideas, news, demonstrate software and hardware and more. Many user groups published their own newsletters and exchanged newsletters with other user groups. Magazines like Sync included listings of groups being formed and established groups, helping extend the reach of the local groups.

There were more than 154 user groups spread across the United States and Canada.
